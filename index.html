WHO WE ARE?

Namaste,


Gorkha Higher Secondary School, one of the reputed and emerging private schools in Nepal, was established in 2011 by Gorkha Education Group to provide quality education for current and future generation of students. It is located at the sound and peaceful panorama of Tulsipur-6, Baruwagaun, Dang covering an area of 3 bigha Land. Gorkha Higher Secondary School has involved Ex-Gurkhas (Lahures), serving armies and local intellectual personalities. The Gorkha Education Group has been running Secondary Schools, Gorkha International Public Higher Secondary School, Ghorahi, Gorkha Higher Secondary School, Tulsipur and Gorkha Model Secondary School, Lamahi, Bachelor College, Victoria International College, Ghorahi, Gorkha Model College, Tulsipur and Swornim College, Lamahi and Gorkha Hospital, Ghorahi.
