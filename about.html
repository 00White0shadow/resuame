WHO WE ARE?

Namaste,

Gorkha Higher Secondary School, one of the reputed and emerging private schools in Nepal, was established in 2011 by Gorkha Education Group to provide quality education for current and future generation of students. It is located at the sound and peaceful panorama of Tulsipur-6, Baruwagaun, Dang covering an area of 3 bigha Land. Gorkha Higher Secondary School has involved Ex-Gurkhas (Lahures), serving armies and local intellectual personalities. The Gorkha Education Group has been running Secondary Schools, Gorkha International Public Higher Secondary School, Ghorahi, Gorkha Higher Secondary School, Tulsipur and Gorkha Model Secondary School, Lamahi, Bachelor College, Victoria International College, Ghorahi, Gorkha Model College, Tulsipur and Swornim College, Lamahi and Gorkha Hospital, Ghorahi.

Gorkha Higher Secondary School, Tulsipur serves over 2500 students. It offers +2 programs in Science and Management stream affiliated to HSEB. It aims to prepare youths creative and productive enough to tackle the growing challenges of the current business and techno world. Regarding the +2 programs and high demand of parents, the school is going to offer two shifts: Day shift and Morning shift in both Science and Management streams.

Gorkha Higher Secondary School, Tulsipur has now become the chosen learning-center for students. Experienced and qualified faculty members, high discipline and dedication to scholarship with a well equipped Physics, Chemistry and Biology Lab, Computer lab, Library, interactive classroom, the state of art teaching methods, educational excursions etc. have definitely fascinated the students to choose this school for study.
